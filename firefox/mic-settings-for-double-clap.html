You will have to grant the extension access to your microphone in order to use the double clap detection mode. Make sure to select "Allow while visiting the site" to avoid interuptions later.

Error accessing microphone. Please grant access to use this feature. Chrome Instructions: 1) Right click the Live Commercial Blocker extension icon in your list of extensions. 2) Select "Manage Extension". 3) Click "Site Settings". 4) Set Microphone to "Allow". 5) Come back to this page and click "Get Started" button again.
Warning! Make sure all background noise reduction, AI noise reduction, conference mode, etc. are turned off on your microphone on your computer. Claps are essentially background noise, so if your computer is using software to filter that out, this mode for the extension will not perform well.
Note: The lower you can get this, while still detecting claps, the better. This will help with reducing false positives.
Warning! If you still aren't detecting claps at this level, it may be due to background noise reduction software running on your computer, as mentioned in the warning above. Search your computer for programs like "Dolby Audio", "Realtek Audio Console", "Acer Purified Voice Console", "HP Audio Control", "Dell Optimizer", or any program that sounds similar. Open the program and disable whatever background noise reduction or AI noise reduction that might be running. Or go into your microphone settings in your system settings and look for any audio enhancement, voice isolation, or noise reduction settings and disable them.

Microphone Tips

While this extension's double clap detection mode has built in ways to exclude noise from loud TVs, it is recommended that if you are pluging your laptop into your TV and choose to use your laptop micophone, try to place your laptop as far away from the TV as you can or use an external microphone placed a decent distance from the TV. This can help with your experience, although not completely necessary. Also, if your external mic has a fuzzy cover over it, it is best to take it off if you can.
